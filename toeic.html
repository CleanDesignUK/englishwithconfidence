About the TOEIC Exam Tentang Ujian TOEIC

The TOEIC exam, officially known as the TOEIC Listening & Reading test, is a standardised English proficiency exam by ETS. Widely recognised by employers and institutions worldwide to assess real-world English skills. Ujian TOEIC, resmi disebut TOEIC Listening & Reading test, adalah ujian kemampuan bahasa Inggris standar oleh ETS. Diakui secara global oleh pemberi kerja dan institusi untuk menilai keterampilan bahasa Inggris dunia nyata.

Our TOEIC preparation materials include authentic mock exams, targeted practice tests, and insider tips for Listening, Reading, Vocabulary, and Grammar.
